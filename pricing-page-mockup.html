Frequently Asked Questions

How does per-user pricing work?
We count active users each month - anyone who asked or answered a question. You're only charged for users who actually engage with the system. Minimum 3 users.
Can I switch between per-user and flat-rate?
Yes! You can switch plans at any time. We'll prorate the difference and credit your account. Most teams start with per-user and upgrade to flat-rate as they grow past 30-40 users.
What happens if I exceed my question limit?
We'll notify you at 80% and 90% of your limit. After that, overage questions cost $0.10 each. Most customers find it more economical to upgrade to the next tier.
What are the three answer detection modes?
Emoji Only: Requires  emoji to mark as answered (strict tracking).
Thread Auto: Any reply automatically marks as answered (casual channels).
Hybrid: Replies stop escalation, but emoji needed for stats (best of both).
Can I try before I buy?
Absolutely! Start with the free tier (no credit card required). When you're ready to upgrade, Professional and Business plans come with a 14-day free trial.
How do refunds work?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales@questionrouter.io with proof of status.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice (NET 30 terms available).
